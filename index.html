Join The Winning Team Today...

We are a closely knitted family of swimmers based in Ilorin, Kwara State. The team is primarily mandated to train students of The University Of Ilorin for competitions. Due to our huge impact so far, we've also taken up the role of training non-students of Unilorin spanning all age groups. We specifically have a younger-age class we train weekly.

Our training sessions are held at Kwara Hotel,G.R.A, Ilorin and we use a standard sized pool for our sessions
